We are the CSUN computer security club called Layer 8. We educate students about computer security and hold friendly competitions to practice skills. Members of the club have competed in National Computer Security Competitions such as Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (CCDC), National Cyber League (NCL), and United States Cyber Challenge capture the flag (CTF) competition.
